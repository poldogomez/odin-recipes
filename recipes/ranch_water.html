Ranch Water

Image source: allrecipes.com

Description

The perfect companion for hot summer days. It's crisp, tart and refreshing with just the right amount of kick.

A true Texan standard that gets it's moniker from the cloudy water that Texas ranchers oftentimes draw out of their ranch wells.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • 1 ice cold bottle of mineral water (preferably Topo Chico)
  • 1 shot of your favorite tequila

Steps

  1. Pop open the bottle of mineral water and pour out 1 shot glass full of water. Drink the shot of water so it doesn't go to waste.
  2. Replace the water you poured out of the bottle with your favorite tequla.
  3. Cut the lime in half and squeeze the juice of half the lime into the bottle.
  4. If you wish, cut the remaining lime into wedges and garnish the neck of the bottle with a lime wedge.
  5. Enjoy responsibly and in good company.
