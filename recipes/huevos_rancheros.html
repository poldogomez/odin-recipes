Huevos Rancheros

Image source: allrecipes.com

Description

An easy and delicious Mexican-style breakfast or brunch, huevos rancheros are an all-time favorite.

You can't go wrong with fried eggs, corn tortillas and spicy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • cookin oil, corn or olive is fine
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 cup fire roasted tomatoes, canned is fine
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, finely chopped
  • jalapeno peppers, to taste, with seeds and veins removed and finely chopped
  • salt
  • ground cummin
  • eggs
  • corn tortillas
  • avocado slices

Steps

  1. Pre-heat sauce pan and add cooking oil. Sautee the onions and garlic until the onion starts to brown.
  2. Add tomatoes, jalapenos, most of the clantro, salt to taste, a pinch of cummin and let simmer.
  3. Pre-heat another sauce pan and add cooking oil. Lighly fry both sides of 2 or 3 corn tortillas. Plate the fried tortillas.
  4. Fry 2 or 3 eggs sunny side up and top the fried tortillas with the eggs.
  5. Top the eggs with the tomato sauce.
  6. Granish with the avocado slices and cilantro.
  7. It can be served with a side of refried beans or crispy breakfast potatoes.
