Peanut Butter, Banana and Honey Sandwich

Image source: closetcooking.com

Description

Looking for a quick and satisfying breakfast or late night snack? Look no further. This easy treat is sure to please.

The combination of soft bread, gooey peanut butter, sweet honey and flavorful banana is hard to pass up.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread. I prefer Portuguese sweet bread, but any soft bread will do. It can be lightly toasted if you wish.
  • peanut butter, any style. I prefer natural creamy salted peanut butter.
  • 1 banana, as ripe as you like it.
  • honey

Steps

  1. Take a generous amount of peanut butter and slather it on a slice of bread.
  2. Cut the peeled banana into as many rounds as you need to completly cover the peanut butter.
  3. Drizzle honey to taste over the bananas.
  4. Top the sandwich with the other slice of bread.
  5. Take your time and enjoy.
